# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Pokemon SA serves mobile and desktop shoppers in Saudi Arabia who want Pokemon cards, sealed product, apparel, magnets, custom cups, and special request ordering. Mobile users are often browsing quickly on a phone, comparing visual product appeal, price, and add-to-cart actions while moving through the store by touch.

## Product Purpose

The storefront helps collectors and fans discover premium Pokemon SA products, understand each product quickly, customize apparel or cups, add items to the cart, and request rare finds. Success means the experience feels collectible, energetic, trustworthy, and fast to shop without hiding price, product imagery, or primary actions.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, electric, collectible. The interface should feel like a premium Pokemon world with dark blue depth, yellow energy, Pokeball cues, character presence, and polished gaming-store motion.

## Anti-references

Do not make the mobile experience feel like stacked desktop cards, a generic ecommerce template, an oversized landing page, or a flat image gallery. Avoid slow browsing, hidden prices, giant product cards, awkward crops, horizontal page drift, and interactions that depend on hover.

## Design Principles

- Mobile is a native shopping surface, not a compressed desktop page.
- Every product moment should quickly reveal image, name, price, and action.
- Preserve the Pokemon SA world while making touch navigation calm, clear, and fast.
- Motion should add energy without causing scroll jank, layout shifts, or intro replay.
- Desktop remains the reference and must stay visually unchanged.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use comfortable touch targets, readable text, stable scroll behavior, reduced-motion fallbacks, and language switching that preserves layout stability across English and Arabic.
